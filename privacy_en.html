AgentBox Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 26, 2026 | Effective Date: June 26, 2026

AgentBox ("the App") is a cross-platform SSH workspace and terminal tool developed by the developer team ("we", "us", or "our"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

1. Core Security & Disclaimer (Important)

As a local-first client tool, the App adheres to the following privacy-first principles:

  • Local Credentials: Your server connection details (including IP addresses, ports, usernames, passwords, SSH private keys) are stored encrypted only on your local device. We never upload or transmit your credentials to our servers.
  • Biometric Data: When you enable biometric authentication (fingerprint/Face ID), the App only invokes the system-secure APIs (e.g., iOS Keychain/Android Keystore). We do not collect and cannot access your biometric data.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We only collect or transmit data in the following essential scenarios:

  • AI Terminal Assistant: When using the AI Assistant, your prompts and CLI context will be sent directly to your configured AI Provider (e.g., Google, OpenAI) for processing. The App itself does not store or analyze these prompts.
  • Network Connectivity: The App requires network permissions to establish SSH/SFTP connections, perform Wake-on-LAN (WOL), and access AI services.
  • Diagnostics Logs: If you experience errors, you may choose to copy and share logs with us to help troubleshoot. Logs contain basic runtime traces and never expose password or private key data.

3. System Permissions

We may request the following permissions only when necessary:

  • Network Access: To connect to remote servers and AI services.
  • Storage / Files Access: To import SSH keys and perform SFTP file transfers.
  • Biometric Auth: To unlock the App secure shield.
  • Notifications: To maintain SSH connection status in the background.

4. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Since this is an account-free, local utility app, all data resides on your device. You can completely erase all data associated with the App by uninstalling it.

If you have questions, please reach out via GitHub Issues.
